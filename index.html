[About]

I build applied AI systems, with a focus on LLM reliability, structured automation, and real-world deployment.

My work centers on turning large language models into usable infrastructure: prompt architectures, multi-model routing, evaluation frameworks, and human-in-the-loop workflows.

Most of my applied work has been in healthcare, where reliability and uncertainty matter. However, the systems I design — structured document parsing, decision support pipelines, and model evaluation harnesses — generalize across domains.

I work at the intersection of statistical modelling, production LLM tooling, and implementation science.

Based in London.

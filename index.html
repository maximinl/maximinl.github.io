Max Lange

Staff Research Scientist on Harley Street | Post-doc @ KCL | Ex-Harvard/MIT

[About]

I mostly work on AI for mental healthcare. I recently completed my PhD at King's College London, where I developed machine learning systems for clinical decision support and drug discovery in psychiatry.

I also work as a Statistician in the Department of Biostatistics & Health Informatics at KCL. My research focuses on building ethical, curious AI systems that can help improve patient outcomes while being mindful of their limitations.

I am a Graduate Member of the British Psychological Society and an Associate Member of the Institution of Engineering and Technology.

A decade ago I was a Quarterback for the Highlands University football team in the US (NCAA DIV II)

Based in London.

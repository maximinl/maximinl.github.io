Dr Max Lange

Post-doc @ KCL | Ex-Harvard/MIT | Research Consultant

[About]

I work on AI for healthcare, with an emphasis on trustworthy clinical decision support and real-world deployment.

I recently completed my PhD at the Institute of Psychiatry, King’s College London, where I developed machine learning systems for clinical decision support and drug discovery.

A lot of my day-to-day work involves LLMs: building tools around them, evaluating them, and translating them into practical workflows.

I’m a Graduate Member of the British Psychological Society and an Associate Member of the Institution of Engineering and Technology.

A decade ago I was a Quarterback for the Highlands University football team in the US (NCAA DIV II). These days always up for a Tennis match (USTA 4.0-ish)

Based in London.
